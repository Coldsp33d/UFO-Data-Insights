  • 1. 0-500 feet above mean sea level

  • 3. Greater than 1,000 feet above mean sea level
The top 10 states with the maximum number of sighitngs in the United States are chosen. The number of sightings for each elevation range for each state can be viewed. The percentage distribution of sightings across all states for each elevation range is also viewable
